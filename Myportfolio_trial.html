Hello everyone, my name is Dung, this year I am 22 years old, my hometown is Ba Vi. Currently, I am a 4th year student at FPT University and majoring in graphic design. I learned a lot of different design styles










UX UI Design


Main job: Designing user experience for website software, mobile app. Work expands when there are more platforms like VR, Smartwatch (something like digital product). Going into depth, working mainly with user needs research, solution sketching, solution testing. There are some jobs that are narrower in scope and revolve around the ease of use and understanding of the product, without going too deep into the above tasks. Main tools used: sketch drawing and sketch simulation tools.
In addition, use a lot of pen and paper and document files. Output: a deployable solution to solve a specific problem. Be it a simple wireframe or prototype or a folder of lots of documents. In general, depending on the problem, the complexity of the solution to be deployed varies. Key thinking: user-centric design



Key skills: creative problem solving UX/UI Designer is probably a combination of the two, so the scope of work is very wide. However, most of the job descriptions that I read are more inclined towards UI Designer than a combination job. So from here on down, I will call it UI Designer instead of UX / UI Designer so that you can better understand the nature of the work. In addition, the job is also divided into 3 environments: advertising agency, product design / construction agency and product company (in-house team).
